Welcome to Logic Systems Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information across all our applications and services.

1. General Principles

Our philosophy is "Privacy by Design". Most of our tools are built to function offline or store data locally on your device. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. App-Specific Details

Wealth Architect

Data Storage: All financial data, investment strategies, and portfolio details are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your financial figures.

Permissions: The app may require internet access for market data updates, but no personal financial info is transmitted.

Smart Routine - LTT

Data Storage: Your habits, routines, and schedules are saved in your device's local database. We believe your daily routine is your business only.

Privacy: No account creation is required to use the core features, ensuring total anonymity.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide a better experience, we may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Required for app functionality and updates.
  • Firebase Analytics: We use anonymized data to understand app crashes and performance issues.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our apps. However, please ensure your device is secured (PIN/Biometrics) to protect the local data stored by our applications.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
