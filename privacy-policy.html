At Logic Systems Studio, we develop tools to help you grow, manage your wealth, and master your routines. We take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how our applications, including Smart Routine - LTT and Wealth Architect, handle your data.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our applications are designed to be privacy-first. Most of the data you enter (habits, financial goals, routines) is stored locally on your device. We do not collect personal identification information unless explicitly provided by you for support purposes.

2. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (for advertisements, where applicable)
  • Firebase Analytics (to improve app performance)

3. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

4. Financial Data (Wealth Architect)

Wealth Architect is a tracking and strategy tool. It does not connect to your bank accounts directly. All financial figures are manually entered and stored on your local device storage.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
